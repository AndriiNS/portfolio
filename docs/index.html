About Me

Personal information:

I've been studying website development for about 2 years now, and I love doing it because you always learn something new and you're always developing. I haven't had a permanent job in this field, but I have done a few custom websites. As I study at the Department of Information Technology, I have tried different languages. Pagination, sliders, tabs, and other things are not a problem for me.

My strengths include adapting to new situations, maintaining a positive attitude, possessing good communication skills, and having a good understanding of English. Currently, my English level is B2, but I am planning to take an English test soon to obtain a C1 certificate.
